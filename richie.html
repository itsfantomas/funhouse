Ricarda Grayson

Ricarda possesses a striking and exotic beauty that sets her apart from the English aristocracy. Her skin is a warm, bronzed hue, contrasting sharply with the pallor of most Victorian ladies. Her eyes are a deep, almost unreal cobalt blue, holding ancient secrets.

Age: 21
Origin: Spain (Hidden Romani Heritage)
Status: Last heir to the Grayson line, betrothed to Lord Bruce Wayne.

She grew up in Spain, believing her mother Maria to be a local, unaware that Maria was Romani hiding under false papers. Ricarda is resourceful, observant, and possesses a quiet determination. She comes to Gotham not for love, but for duty—and perhaps, survival.

The Contract

The marriage contract, sealed before Bruce Wayne's birth, promised the future Duke to the Grayson heiress. It was a desperate bid by John Grayson's father to save the family from ruin. Neither Thomas Wayne nor John Grayson suspected their lines might be linked to the Court of Owls.

Recently, John and Maria were murdered in Spain. Ricarda fled to Gotham. The union is unequal, burdened by duty, hidden pasts, and the complex interplay between two vastly different souls.

"Beware the Court of Owls, that watches all the time..."
